Our Gins of the Month

Every month, we send our club members a full-sized bottle of amazing craft gin from one of the world’s finest distillers, in a treat-filled Gin of the Month box.

Our gins include rare and even exclusive editions not available elsewhere that are created specially for our Craft Gin Club members. We capture the passion of our craft distiller partners in our monthly GINNED! Magazine, packed with real-life gin stories, tailored cocktail recipes and much more.

AUGUST 2018

Dry Island Gin

View this post on Instagram

Last month my #Ginscovery adventure took a huge step forward when I was made aware of the wonder that is @craftginclub I checked it out and signed up immediately for a monthly subscription of gin and assorted accompaniments delivered to my door... sounds ideal! . . Having got married earlier this month and jetting straight off to Mexico on honeymoon, I was not home when my first package arrived so am late to the party in trying and reviewing August’ offering but my goodness was it worth the wait! . . Upon opening the package I was greeted by a 70cl bottle of Dry Island Gin, a collaboration between highly respected Australian distillers @fourpillarsgin and award winning Swedish distiller @hernogin There was also a couple bottles of @fentimansltd Valencian Orange tonic, which I was excited to try as well as @greenandblacks chocolates and @tyrrellsofficial #poshcorn as well as Nut Shots and a @fourpillarsgin Cocktail Book (No. 3) for good measure! I was delighted with my first package! . . So, what is this collaboration gin like? Quite simply, it’s delicious! The notes for it describe it as being distilled with juniper, coriander, river mint, roasted wattleseed, strawberry gum, lemon myrtle, meadowsweet and fresh lemons. To me, the combination of the gin, the valencian tonic and a few strawberries for garnish (courtesy of my brother-in-law as I had nothing in after honeymoon) was just a perfect summer blend and complimented each ingredient perfectly. I will be trying this with many combinations but this will be hard to beat! . . Unfortunately, as it is part of a one-off distiller series, there is only a small amount of this gin produced and even less available in England so I will savour it but I would recommend to you to not only hunt it down but try the valencian tonic wherever you may find that too! . . So thank you @craftginclub for this wonderful delivery... I am eager for Septembers to arrive already! #FourPillarsGin #HernöGin #gin #tonic #gintonic #ginfluencer #distillery #craftgin #craftginclub #ginscovery #ginstagram #gincredible #ginspiration #gintastic
